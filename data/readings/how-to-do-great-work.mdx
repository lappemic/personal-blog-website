## My Notes

This essay explores the common patterns behind doing great work across different fields.

Key takeaways:

- Choose work that you find genuinely interesting
- Work on your own projects, not just what others expect
- Be curious and follow your interests persistently
- The best ideas often come from the intersection of fields
